Kevin Na Driving Analysis


Kevin Na is one of the shortest players on tour when it comes to driving distance. He is considerably below average year over year on total distance with his driver.
He makes up for his lost distance by being more accurate than the average player on tour. He has not been able to crack the top 20 in his career.
While Kevin Na hits fairways more consistantly than the average player, he would benefit greatly from increased distance.
